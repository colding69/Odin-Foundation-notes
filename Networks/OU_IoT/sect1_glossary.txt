Converged networks: 
Definition: When {{c1::information of different types}} (broadband data, voice, video etc) {{c1::all use same communication channel and network structure}}.

LAN:
Stands for {{c1::Local Area Network}}
Definition: {{c1::Covers end devices in a limited coverage area}}.

WAN:
Stands for {{c1::Wide Area Network}}
Definition: {{c1::Internconnects multiple LAN over a broad geographical area}}.

DCN
Stands for {{c1::Data Center Network}}
Exchanges data {{c1::quickly}}.
Used in data centers.



End devices:
Definition: {{c1::Source and destination of messages transmitted over a network}}.

Hosts:
Definition: {{c1::End devices in a network participating in communication}}.

Intermediary devices:
Definition: {{c1::Computing device which interconnects individual hosts to the network or connects networks to each other}}.
Function: {{c1: determines path and directs data}} inside network.
Examples: {{c1::Routers}}, {{c1::firewalls}}

Things:
Definition: {{c1::physical objects with sensors that are connected to a network}}.

Server: 
Definition: {{c1::A type of end device that provides information to client.}}

Client: 
Definition: {{c1:: A type of end device makes requests to and displays information from server.}}
